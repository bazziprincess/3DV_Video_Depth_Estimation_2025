Abstract

We present VDE, a novel architecture for monocular and stereo-based video depth estimation. Our method leverages temporal consistency and spatial cues across frames to recover accurate and dense depth maps from video sequences. With a modular pipeline and attention-based refinement, VDE achieves competitive performance on multiple datasets while maintaining real-time inference capability.
